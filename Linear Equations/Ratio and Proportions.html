Ratio and Proportions

Fraction Definition

One or more of the equal parts of any thing are called a Fraction.

The equal parts in the definition implies linearity, that is  \(\frac 1 n *n = 1\)

Proportion is fractions that are equivalent.

First steps : Arithmetic Ratio's to Algebra

Example : Two-thirds of what number is 4?

Understanding the problem in arithmetic sense

This question in arithmetic embeds the concept of Linear equation, in the fact that it is asking for how many times should two-third's be added to make it 4 ? The answer could then be arrived as  \(4 \div \frac 2 3\).  But there are other equivalent representations to do it as well 

Understanding the problem in proportional sense

This problem translates to find the fourth proportional  of    \(\frac 2 3 : 1 :: 4 : ?\)

An this can be solved in multiple equivalent ways

  •  \(( 1 \div \frac 2 3)*4\)
  •    \((4*3)/2\)
  • (\((4/2)*3\)
